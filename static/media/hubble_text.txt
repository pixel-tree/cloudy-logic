"What am I looking at?" You might ask yourself.
Or, you might already be familiar with this infamous photograph captured by the Hubble Space Telescope.
You are staring at the Hubble Deep Field; and you are in fact looking back in time, at 13 billion years of cosmological history. The image is a timeline of the universe: an intricate tapestry of otherwise ephemeral narratives (on a cosmic scale) but immortalised here in pixels.
